Property Data Cube
Redefining Data Intelligence with Property Data Cube
Property Data Cube stands as the premier provider of multi-family contacts data, offering unparalleled sales and marketing data intelligence for property data acquisition. Leveraging the expertise and experience of Info Analytica, a leading data intelligence firm, Property Data Cube empowers businesses with comprehensive B2B demand generation, marketing, and sales intelligence services. The platform enables users to enhance their understanding of prospects and clients by consolidating data to create richer profiles, while also providing tools to evaluate and optimize data accuracy.
Challenge
Creating a website that reflects Property Data Cube’s values clearly and seamlessly integrates with its brand. The website should serve conveying the intricacies of multi-family contacts data and data intelligence services in a clear and understandable manner. With a focus on diverse audiences, the site should offer clear and compelling information, presenting the company's culture in an accessible tone. The ultimate aim is to craft an engaging, standout website that aligns closely with Property Data Cube’s unique voice and identity. They needed a platform that showcased their database capabilities in helping clients find more opportunities and connect with decision-makers in the multi-family housing industry. Additionally, they wanted to establish credibility in the market, attract new clients, and serve as a valuable resource for existing ones.
Deliverables
  • Logo Design
  • Rebranding
  • Collaterals
  • Design Mockups
  • Content Strategy
  • Iconography
  • Development Assets
Approach
The initial phase involved comprehensive research into the needs and challenges of Property Data Cube's target market. This encompassed studying the requirements of telecom providers, security equipment vendors, maintenance services providers, smart meter providers, real estate agents, and other key stakeholders in the multi-family housing sector. Following this, a targeted content strategy was crafted to emphasize Property Data Cube's core services. This strategy included clear explanations of their offerings, benefits tailored to different client segments, and real-life examples showcasing the impact of their data intelligence solutions. The website was then designed with a sleek, contemporary look that reflects the professionalism and trustworthiness of Property Data Cube. User experience was a key focus, ensuring easy navigation for visitors to swiftly access the information they sought.
RESULTS
The website effectively positions Property Data Cube as a leading provider of multi-family contacts data, enhancing brand recognition in the market. The integration of Info Analytica's expertise and client testimonials bolstered Property Data Cube's credibility as a leading provider of data intelligence solutions. Clear and compelling content, along with intuitive navigation, has resulted in increased user engagement and longer time spent on the site. The Property Data Cube website stands as a testament to their expertise and commitment to providing top-notch data intelligence solutions for the multi-family housing sector. It effectively communicates their value proposition, attracts new clients, and supports ongoing business growth.
